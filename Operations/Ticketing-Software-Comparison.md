# 🎫 Ticketing Software Comparison & Recommendation

## 📌 Introduction
As an IT Administrator for CycleIT Ltd (approx. 400 staff including onsite and offsite workers), there is a need for an efficient ticketing system to manage day-to-day support requests from staff and customers.

This document evaluates three ticketing systems and recommends the most suitable solution.

---

## 📂 Software Options

### 🔹 Freshdesk
Freshdesk is a cloud-based ticketing system known for its simple and user-friendly interface.

**Key Features:**
- Easy-to-use interface  
- Multi-channel support (email, chat, phone)  
- Ticket management  
- Suitable for small to medium businesses  

---

### 🔹 Zendesk Support
Zendesk is a web-based SaaS (Software as a Service) platform widely used for customer support.

**Key Features:**
- Cloud-based (no installation required)  
- Multi-channel communication (email, chat, phone, social media)  
- Advanced automation and workflows  
- Strong reporting and analytics  
- Highly scalable  

---

### 🔹 Zoho Desk
Zoho Desk is a flexible and cost-effective ticketing system with good customisation options.

**Key Features:**
- Customisable interface  
- Multi-channel support  
- Automation features  
- Affordable pricing  
- Suitable for small to medium businesses  

---

| Feature        | Freshdesk        | Zendesk        | Zoho Desk      |
|---------------|------------------|----------------|----------------|
| Cost          | Medium           | Medium         | Low            |

| Automation    | Basic            | Advanced       | Good           |
| Scalability   | Good             | Excellent      | Good           |

---

## ✅ Final Recommendation: Zendesk

Zendesk is recommended as the best solution for CycleIT Ltd due to its scalability, automation capabilities, and multi-channel support.

**Reasons for Selection:**
- Scalable for growing organisations  
- Clean and user-friendly interface  
- Advanced automation to improve efficiency  
- Centralised communication platform  
- Strong reporting and analytics  
- Multi-language support  

---

Ticketing systems like Zendesk are widely used in IT environments to manage support requests efficiently, improve response times, and enhance customer experience.
