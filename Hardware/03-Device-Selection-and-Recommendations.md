# 💼 Device Selection and Recommendations

## 📌 Overview

In this section, appropriate hardware devices were selected for different roles within an organisation.

The goal was to choose systems that meet performance requirements while staying within budget.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Marketing Manager Device

### 📌 Selected Device

**HP ProBook 450 G9 Business Laptop**

---

### 📊 Specifications

- Processor: Intel Core i7-1255U (12th Gen)  
- Speed: Up to 4.7 GHz  
- RAM: 32 GB  
- Storage: 512 GB NVMe SSD  
- Display: 15.6”  
- Graphics: Intel Iris Xe  
- Weight: 1.74 kg  

---

### 🎯 Justification

- Lightweight and portable for office and remote work  
- Strong CPU performance for multitasking  
- 32GB RAM supports multiple applications  
- Fast NVMe SSD improves responsiveness  

👉 Suitable for business and marketing tasks.

---

## 👨‍💻 Senior Developer Device

### 📌 Selected Device

**Custom High-Performance Desktop PC**

---

### 📊 Specifications

- CPU: Intel Core i9-13900K  
- RAM: 64 GB DDR5  
- Storage: 2TB NVMe + 2TB SSD  
- GPU: RTX 3060 (12GB)  
- Motherboard: Z790 chipset  
- PSU: 1000W  

---

### 🎯 Justification

- High-performance CPU for development and compiling  
- Large RAM supports heavy workloads and virtual machines  
- Fast storage improves development speed  
- Dedicated GPU supports advanced tasks  

👉 Ideal for software development and demanding workloads.

---

## 🧑‍💼 Receptionist Device

### 📌 Selected Device

**Custom Mid-Range Desktop PC**

---

### 📊 Specifications

- CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 7600X  
- RAM: 32 GB  
- Storage: 1 TB SSD  
- GPU: GTX 1050 Ti  
- PSU: 650W  

---

### 🎯 Justification

- Suitable performance for daily office tasks  
- Fast SSD improves system responsiveness  
- Supports multitasking (emails, calls, scheduling)  
- Cost-effective solution  

👉 Meets requirements without unnecessary cost.

| Role | Device Type | Performance Level | Purpose |
